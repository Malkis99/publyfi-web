# Security & Compliance

PublyFi is built on a foundation of trust, transparency, and regulatory readiness. As a global `Web3` platform merging entertainment with finance, PublyFi ensures every user, partner, and investor participates in a legally sound, secure, and auditable ecosystem.

## 1. Regulatory Alignment
PublyFi adheres to international standards, aligning with:
*   `GDPR` (EU) — full compliance for data protection & user rights
*   `AML` / `CFT` (`FATF` Guidelines) — anti-money laundering, counter-terrorism financing
*   `KYC` Regulations — multi-tier verification through trusted providers
*   Digital Asset Frameworks: `MiCA` (EU), `FinCEN` (US), `MAS` (SG), `VARA` (UAE)
*   Data Residency: servers in compliant jurisdictions with regional backups

> Continuous legal audits ensure PublyFi adapts to evolving `Web3` regulations globally.

## 2. AML Policy
To prevent misuse and ensure transparency:
*   **Source of Funds:** verified via `KYC` partner `APIs`.
*   **Transaction Monitoring:** `AI`-driven anomaly detection.
*   **Wallet Screening:** cross-checked with sanctions lists (`OFAC`, `EU`, `UN`).
*   **Suspicious Activity Reports (`SARs`):** automated escalation to compliance team.
*   **Withdrawal Limits:** tiered by verification level.
*   **Partner Due Diligence:** all integrations vetted for `AML` adherence.

> PublyFi’s AML engine ensures every transaction is traceable, auditable, and legitimate.

## 3. Legal Structure & DAO Governance
*   Operates under compliant entity with `VASP` registration.
*   **DAO Wrapper:** recognized legal structure enabling decentralized governance.
*   **Transparent Treasury:** all fund flows visible on-chain.
*   **Smart Contract Audits:** independent third-party assessments pre-launch.
*   **Liability Shield:** `DAO` voting and proposals governed by on-chain logic.

> Legal-first `DAO` ensures community power within safe jurisdictional boundaries.

## 4. Risk Management
PublyFi implements multi-dimensional risk controls:
*   **Technical Risks:** code audits, redundancy, bug bounties.
*   **Economic Risks:** controlled token emissions, burn policy, `DAO` oversight.
*   **Operational Risks:** access controls, segregation of duties.
*   **Counterparty Risks:** partner vetting, continuous monitoring.

> Every layer — from code to community — includes built-in risk mitigations.

## 5. Investor & Partner Assurance
For investors and enterprise partners:
*   **Regular Reporting:** quarterly transparency reports (treasury, users, emissions).
*   **Audited Financials:** verified by licensed audit firms.
*   **Compliance Certificates:** `KYC`/`AML`, data security, and smart contract verifications.
*   **Dispute Resolution:** governed under local laws with arbitration clauses.

> Institutional-grade compliance for sustainable growth.

## 6. Ethical Governance
PublyFi is committed to responsible innovation:
*   **Fair Access:** no pay-to-win mechanics, transparent reward logic.
*   **User Rights:** data portability, deletion, export on request.
*   **Transparency:** open-source policies, `DAO` decisions visible.
*   **Equality:** inclusive content policies, zero discrimination.

> PublyFi doesn’t just meet compliance — it sets new ethical standards for `Web3`.

## Summary
By combining regulatory compliance, legal clarity, and ethical governance, PublyFi ensures:
*   User data and funds are fully protected.
*   Token economy operates transparently.
*   `DAO` governance remains lawful and auditable.
*   Partners and investors engage with confidence.

> PublyFi = `Web3` trust, verified.
