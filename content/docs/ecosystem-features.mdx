Ecosystem Features
Core Modules

Streams: High-quality live content with creator tools.

Reels: Short-form highlights and edits that fuel discovery.

Quests: Creator-defined challenges; users earn $PUBL and NFTs.

Marketplace: Trade cosmetics, avatars, skins; fusion & upgrades.

DAO Governance: Token-weighted proposals and votes.

Subscriptions: Free, Prime ($15 one-time), Pro ($15/month).

GameSync (Loyalty Engine)
Tracks watch time, quest completions, trades, and achievements to grow a living profile.

Avatars & Customization
Visual identity evolves through NFTs, cosmetics, and seasonal progress.

Growth Loops
Quests → Rewards → Sharing → Marketplace → Reputation → New Quests.
